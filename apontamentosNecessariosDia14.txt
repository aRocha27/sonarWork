Why a hybrid approach is preferable to multi-class YOLO in sonar imagery

End-to-end multi-class learning in sonar imagery is challenged by strong speckle noise, acoustic shadowing, and the lack of well-defined object boundaries. By decoupling detection from classification, the proposed hybrid approach leverages data-driven localization while enforcing physically meaningful criteria based on acoustic intensity.
